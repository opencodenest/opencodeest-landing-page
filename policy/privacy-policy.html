OpenCodeNest Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2025

1. Introduction

OpenCodeNest is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. We may collect certain data as you interact with our website or contribute to our projects. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of your data as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal information, such as your name and email address, when you register or contribute.
  • Usage data, such as IP addresses, browser types, and pages visited, to improve our services.
  • Contributions and activity on repositories, including pull requests, issues, and comments.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve OpenCodeNest’s services.
  • Communicate with you about your contributions and participation.
  • Ensure compliance with our Terms and Conditions and community guidelines.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted partners for the purposes of delivering our services.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for data collection where applicable.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and revise the “Effective Date” at the top. Your continued use of OpenCodeNest services signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
